Story


Mrs. Satarupa Majumder completed her M. Com and then specialized in Pre-Primary & Primary Education from Assembly of God Church, Kolkata. She taught at leading private schools in Kolkata for 15+ years. She has a deep understanding of pedagogy and has attended several workshops on Pre-Primary & Primary Education by TLI-Pedagogies. She has been a member of Progressive Educational Teachers Society under Loreto, Kolkata, and a member of Jolly Phonics, London. Satarupa started visiting Hingalganj, Sundarbans every weekend to teach children. Slowly started training village women to teach, and eventually had six resource persons-cum-teachers at the village whom she would pay from her own pocket.

THE FOUNDER STORY

Strengthening the foundation literacy and numeracy of these children was critical for their future and so in 2012 Satarupa developed an integrated model of Montessori and experiential learning to make education more accessible, fun, and activity-based for rural children. She set up Katakhali Swapnopuron Welfare Society along with other like-minded individuals. Her journey has been documented in prestigious papers like The Hindu, Bloomberg, Better India and Global Indian for her exemplary work in arena of educational and community empowerment of the Sunderbans.
